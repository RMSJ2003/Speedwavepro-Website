About SPEEDWAVE

SPEEDWAVE is one of the most trusted Lights and Sounds Supplier in Metro Manila and CALABARZON and other provinces. 15 years of High Quality and Professional Service with thousands of Event Experiences

SPEEDWAVE provides high quality professional sounds system, lightings, LED Video Wall, Staging services, Dome Roofing, Barricades, and Rentals for concerts, festival, weddings, parties and events of all types, small to large. We are proud to boast that we have one of the LARGEST HIGH END equipment in rental inventory when it comes to lights, sound system and Staging services.
